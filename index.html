About Me

I am an experienced designer who wears a lot of hats. I've worked in graphic design, web design/development, marketing, virtual reality, and video/animation. And a lot of other things. In other words, I'm constantly trying to learn new things.

I am a creative thinker - I have a vivid imagination and a keen eye for detail, but I am also very analytical and methodical. In college, I double-majored in computer science with an arts emphasis, and in philosophy with a science emphasis, appeasing both the designer and the thinker in me.

I am a problem-solver. I thrive in collaborative environments, especially with interdepartmental projects, and I love the challenge of turning complex problems into beautiful, intuitive solutions. The best part of every project: getting to witness it have a positive impact on someone's life.

I love my work, and I am thrilled to be sharing it with you. Please don't hesitate to reach out to me at nvanmaerssen@gmail.com with any questions or if you think I can be of service to you. In the meantime, enjoy perusing my handiwork!
